About Me

I am a student of the University of Utah's Coding Bootcamp. I currently work as a Personal Finance Counselor in the nonprofit sector. I assist clients with establishing personal budgets, evaluating debt loads, setting financial goals, and developing a plan of action. Before my current job, I traveled the country performing service work with AmeriCorps - examples of service projects I worked are were building houses with Habitat for Humanity, building hiking trails in the Rocky Mountains, building ropes courses for 4H, and filing taxes for low-income individuals under VITA.
