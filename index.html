About Me

I'm a student of the University of Utah's Coding Bootcamp. I currently work in the nonprofit sector as a Personal Finance Counselor. I assist clients with establishing personal budgets, evaluating debt loads, setting financial goals, and developing a plan of action. Before this, I traveled the country with AmeriCorps performing service work for various nonprofits - some of the projects I= worked on in AmeriCorps included rebuilding houses destroyed by tornados in Oklahoma with Habitat for Humanity,building hiking trails in the Rockies, building ropes courses in Arkansas for 4H,and filing taxes for low-income individuals and families with VITA in Kansas City.
